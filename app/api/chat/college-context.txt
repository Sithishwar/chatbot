Overview:
College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG) is one of the premier engineering institutions in India and functions as a constituent campus of Anna University, Chennai. The institution has a long academic heritage dating back to 1794, making it one of the oldest technical education establishments in Asia. Over the years, it has evolved into a leading center for engineering education, research, and innovation.

CEG operates under Anna University and follows the university's academic regulations, curriculum structure, and examination system. It serves as a hub for undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple engineering and technology disciplines.

MISSION AND EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVE
---------------------------------
The primary mission of CEG is to provide high-quality technical education that combines strong theoretical foundations with practical application. The institution aims to:

- Develop technically competent graduates.
- Promote research and innovation in engineering fields.
- Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration.
- Support industry-oriented learning.
- Foster ethical responsibility and societal contribution.
